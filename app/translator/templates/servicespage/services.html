Speech-to-Text and Text-to-Speech

Speech/Text translation

The speech-to-text and text-to-speech functionalities could be integrated into each feature, allowing users to input text or speech and display the results of speech-to-text or text-to-speech conversion. For example, the user could speak their insurance policy number, and the application would convert it to text and display it on the screen.

What we offer;

Emergency Services

If the user selects the Emergency Services feature, the application could present a screen with a list of emergency service providers such as ambulance, police, or fire department. Each provider could be represented by a card that displays their contact information and location. The user could have the option to call for help directly from the application.

OUR SERVICES

Insurance Wallet

If the user selects the Insurance Wallet feature, the application could present a screen with a list of the user's insurance policies. Each policy could be represented by a card that displays the policy number, coverage details, and any pending claims. The user could also have the option to add new policies, submit claims, and make payments.
